Step 2: Clear Cookies (Manual)

Due to browser security, cookies must be cleared manually. Here's how:

  • Chrome/Edge: Press F12 → Application tab → Cookies → Delete all for localhost:1313 and auth.scarsdalepack440.org
  • Firefox: Press F12 → Storage tab → Cookies → Delete all
  • Safari: Develop menu → Show Web Inspector → Storage → Cookies → Delete all

Or simply: Use an Incognito/Private window for testing (recommended!)
